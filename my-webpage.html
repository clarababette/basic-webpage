Dream Destination

Dream City exists for about a month each (non-pandemic) year. It is a collection of camps at the Roskilde music festival in Denmark. It is run as a collective where you take responsibility for each other and the environment. It is also the only camping area at the festival in which you can build your own structure.

As building things and music are high up on the list of things I love, I think Dream City would be a pretty great place to be.

A Useful Foreign Language

Learning a language can be a long and tedious process. If I were to learn a foreign language, I would choose the language based on how useful it would be and how quickly I could reach a conversational level. And so… I would like to learn German.

According to the WEF Power Language Index, German is the seventh most useful language. English, being the global lingua franca, is of course ranked first.

You may wonder why I would choose to learn the seventh most useful language. I have previously ventured into the world of Romance languages with dubious success and so maybe I should stick with Germanic languages. For now…

Historical Places in Cape Town

District 6 Museum

  • If you arrange ahead, you can walk through the museum with a former resident.
  • The museum has a large audio archive containing recording of area's oral history.
  • Much of the museum's work takes place within the community and is not bound by the building.

Parliament of the Republic of South Africa

  • The Artworks Office of Parliament is responsible for the maintance of almost 4000 artistic and heritage items.
  • Although currently suspended due to the pandemic, you are able to observe both National Assembly and National Council of Provences debates from the public gallery.
  • The President is not a member of Parliament and does not have a vote in the National Assembly.

Iziko Museum

  • The museum is home to more than one and a half million scientific specimens.
  • After visiting the museum you can catch a show at the Planetarium next door.
  • The scientists that work at the museum can be called upon as expert witnesses in legal cases.
